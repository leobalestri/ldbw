(people v. kelly (2006) 40 cal.4th 106, 109110; people v. wende (1979) 25 cal.3d 436, 441.) eligibility for resentencing under penal code section 1170.18 is limited to convictions for the offenses specified therein commitment offenses are within the scope of that section. disposition the judgment is affirmed. not to be published.. lui, j. we concur: chaney, acting p. j. johnson, j.
