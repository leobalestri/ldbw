(a)(1).) defendant brandished a knife at sandra perez on october 8, 2013. the trial court suspended imposition of sentence and placed defendant on three years probation. we appointed counsel to represent defendant on appeal. after reviewing the record, appointed appellate counsel filed an opening brief in which no issues were raised. instead, appointed appellate counsel requested this court independently review the entire record on appeal pursuant to people v. wende (1979) 25 cal.3d 436, 441.
