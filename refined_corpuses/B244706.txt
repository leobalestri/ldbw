(a)). the court sentenced sanchez to a state prison term of 18 years 8 months: (1) the upper term of 7 years on count 7, plus the upper term of 10 years for the section 12022.5, subdivision (a), enhancement; and (2) a consecutive term of 1 year 8 months (one-third the 5-year midterm) on count 8. the court dismissed all remaining counts and special allegations. sanchez filed a notice of appeal, contending that [t]his appeal is based on the sentence or other matters occurring after the plea that do not affect the validity of the plea. we appointed counsel to represent sanchez in the matter. after examining the record, counsel filed a wende brief raising no issues on appeal and requesting that we independently review the record.
