(a)). defendant is a sex offender subject to registration requirements. on january 25, 2017, he was released from jail, after an incarceration lasting more than 30 days, and he failed to re-register. he was initially charged with felony failure to re-register, but agreed to a negotiated plea to the misdemeanor. he entered the plea on november 19, 2018, was convicted, and appealed shortly thereafter. he sought a certificate of probable cause, seeking to withdraw his plea, claiming he had not been fully informed of all issues surrounding his plea. the court denied his request for a certificate of probable cause.
