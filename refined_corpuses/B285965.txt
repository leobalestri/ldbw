(a)), and admitted allegations that he had personally used and discharged a firearm (pen. code,  12022.53, subd. (b)) and caused great bodily injury (pen. code,  12022.7, subd. (a)) in connection with that offense. his plea was part of an agreement with the prosecution that called for a 16-year sentence as follows: the low term of three years for the carjacking, plus three years for the bodily injury allegation and 10 years for the firearm use allegation. in exchange, three counts of assault with a firearm (pen. code,  245, subd.
