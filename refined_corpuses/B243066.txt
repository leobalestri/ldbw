(jennings v. marralle (1994) 8 cal.4th 121, 126; olson v. cory (1983) 35 cal.3d 390, 398.) we issued an order to show cause and calendared the matter for oral argument. the post-judgment motion argued that defendant did not receive the effective assistance of counsel in connection with the removal of a spectator. the july 20, 2012 post-judgment order is not appealable on that ground. (people v. cantrell (1961) 197 cal.app.2d 40, 43; people v. bowles (1933) 135 cal.app. 514, 516; see people v. thomas (1959) 52 cal.2d 521, 527; 6 witkin & epstein, cal. criminal law (4th ed. -342.) further, insofar as the july 20, 2012 post-judgment order may be construed as one denying a habeas corpus petition, it is not appealable.
