(smith v. robbins (2000) 528 u.s. 259, 277-284 [120 s.ct. 746, 145 l.ed.2d 756]; people v. kelly (2006) 40 cal.4th 106, 118-119; people v. wende (1979) 25 cal.3d 436, 441-442.) the trial court correctly ruled that first degree burglary is not one of the theft offenses for which a defendant may seek reclassification pursuant to penal code section 1170.18, subdivision (a). 3 disposition the order is affirmed. perluss, p. j. we concur: zelon, j. bensinger, j.* * judge of the los angeles superior court, assigned by the chief justice pursuant to article vi, section 6 of the california constitution.
