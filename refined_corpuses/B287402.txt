(see people v. espinoza (2018) 22 cal.app.5th 794, 799.) on august 24, 2018, brief pursuant to people v. wende (1979) 25 cal.3d 436 (wende). the brief included a declaration that counsel would write to defendant, explaining the brief counsel was filing, and informing defendant of his right to file a supplemental brief. this court sent defendant a notice advising him that a wende brief had been filed and that he had 30 days to submit a brief or letter raising any issues he wished us to consider. defendant did not file a supplemental brief. we have examined the entire record and are satisfied that orney has fully complied with his responsibilities and that no arguable issues exist.
